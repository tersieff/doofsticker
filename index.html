A Simple Roof Rack Warning Sticker

Doof Sticker is a vinyl window cling that adheres to the inside of your windshield that warns you about the height of the gear on your roof, helping remind you to not drive under low clearance areas.

Doof Sticker is customizable for your specific use. Write in the spaces provided what you have on your roof along with the total height of your vehicle plus all your gear up top.

Clings to Your Windshield

Doof Sticker is designed to be placed on the inside of your windshield, in a place that will not block your vision, but will still be a visual reminder that you have gear on top of your roof. Doof Sticker vinyl cling does not use adhesive, so it can be removed and re-used, leaving no mess on your windows.

So wether it's bikes, snowboards, skis, surfboards, paddleboards, kayaks, canoes, or cargo boxes on your roof rack, Doof Sticker helps save your gear from low clearance damage.
