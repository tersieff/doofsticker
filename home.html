A Simple Roof Rack Warning Sticker

Doof Sticker is a vinyl window cling that adheres to the inside of your windshield that warns you about the height of the gear on your roof, helping you not drive under low clearance areas.

Write in the spaces provided what you have on your roof along with the total height of your vehicle with all your gear up top.

Clings to Your Windshield

Peel your Doof Sticker off its backing and position it on your windshield where it will be a visual reminder, but does not block your vision.
